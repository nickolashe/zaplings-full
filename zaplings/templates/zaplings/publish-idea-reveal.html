Heads up! Your idea is about to be public

A couple things to know before you proceed:

It's not "your" idea until you do it. Chances are other people have the idea too. Sharing your idea is a great way to keep moving forward and build a great team.

If you want to get a patent, be careful about the information you share on your idea page. An idea itself cannot be patented, only the execution of an idea, so details such as product design or technical processes should only be shared with individuals you trust.
