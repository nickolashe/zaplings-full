30 seconds: What do you offer?

Adding tags with the microphone

Follow these guidelines to quickly share what you love, offer, or need:

Limit tags to 3 words or less. Shorter tags are selected by more people, which helps us match you more easily.

Say "and" between tags. This lets us know where one tag ends and another begins. Avoid using other filler words between tags. (You can say "I love", "I offer", or "I need" between tags too.)

Speak clearly, and take your time. This improves voice recognition accuracy. (If it doesn't work, blame Google.)

Check for accuracy. Once the microphone stops, you will see your tags added. For any incorrect tags, unclick that tag and type the correct tag in the input box.
