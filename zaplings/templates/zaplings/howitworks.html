How it works

Our mission at Zaplings is to help you create. Here's how we're doing it:

Private idea book

Keep all your ideas in one place.

Plant an idea seed

Share a brief description of your idea with Zaplings and on Twitter to seek support or to donate it to the community.

Create an idea page

Intorduce your idea in more detail and share the page with friends.

Browse public ideas

Find ideas you love, give feedback, and connect with creators.

Be the Connector

Introduce friends to Zaplings and to each other to become a Gold Connector. We will match you to other Connectors to grow your network stronger.

Get introduced

As the community grows, we will suggest conversations with people who share your passion and offer what you need.
